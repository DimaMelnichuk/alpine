SEA DIVING
31 Jul, 2020

PADI Entry level dive courses offer an easy and flexible way to learn diving skills. Each course is part of the PADI system, so you can decide how many modules you choose to learn, without losing ‘dive credits’ for your next dive adventure. Try a dive for just half a day or take the plunge and sign up for the full open water course or why not start a new adventure from the comfort of your own sofa? Learn to dive with PADI e-learning Open Water Course

Sharm el Sheikh is a fantastic holiday resort, packed full of adventures and things to do. One of the top activities you can participate in is SCUBA diving. The Red Sea is lapping at your toes, so take the plunge and learn to dive in Egypt with one of our entry level PADI courses.

Let the whole family have a fantastic holiday in Egypt and all leave with the adventure of a PADI course and with Red Sea SCUBA memories.

The Red Sea Diving College can offer a variety of PADI courses for beginners to suit every need. Choose from a one day PADI Discover the Red Sea adventure, sign up for the PADI Scuba diver or a full license to dive PADI Open Water course.
